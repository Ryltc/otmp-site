The Mission

At On The Map Productions, our mission is simple, to put you on the map. In our 10 year plan we have a goal to be a fully functioning record label. The new age is streaming, and artists don’t make much money doing that. So it is our belief that each artist under On The Map Productions has the ability to fully appreciate the value of their efforts. Meaning, you get out what you put in. Our brand grows, and the OTMP family helps to lead in the new era artistry.

The Studio

On The Map Productions LLC, or OTMP is based in the East End of Waterbury CT. Our brand new studio is designed to be a one stop shop. We track, edit, mix, and master any and all audio. We run sessions using Apple, Universal Audio, Avid, and Yamaha hardware with Native Instruments, IK Media, Celemony, Izotope, and Avid softwares. In the past we have worked on studio albums, live shows, and music for film. We look forward to bringing your music or audio to life, as we strive to put you On The Map.
