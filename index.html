The Studio

    On The Map Productions LLC, or OTMP is based in the East End of Waterbury CT. Our brand new studio is designed to be a one stop shop. We track, edit, mix, and master any and all audio. We run sessions using Apple, Universal Audio, Avid, and Yamaha hardware with Native Instruments, IK Media, Celemony, Izotope, and Avid softwares. In the past we have worked on full albums, live shows, EPs, singles, and even music for a feature film. We look forward to bringing your music or audio to life, as we strive to put you On The Map.


The Engineer

    Eli, or also known by his stagename, RYLTK (Pronounced: Real Talk, or Realize Your Life Through Knowledge), has been playing music for over 20 years, and producing audio for 10 years. As a graduate of SAE's audio technology program in CA, he moved to CT where he helps out at llnnll records and Saints Temple Studios as the chief audio engineer. When he's not in his studio's control room, tracking, editing, mixing, or mastering, he's behind the mic deliverying consious minded lyrics loaded with impactual messages that dive into todays civil and political issues. You can find him on facebook, instagram, and twitter, SoundCloud, and YouTube.
