The Studio

On The Map Productions LLC, or OTMP is based in the East End of Waterbury CT. Our brand new studio is designed to be a one stop shop. We track, edit, mix, and master any and all audio. We run sessions using Apple, Universal Audio, Avid, and Yamaha hardware with Native Instruments, IK Media, Celemony, Izotope, and Avid softwares. In the past we have worked on full albums, live shows, EPs, singles, and even music for a feature film. We look forward to bringing your music or audio to life, as we strive to put you On The Map.
