On The Map Productions LLC

The Label

On The Map Productions is CT's newest record label for artists and creators. We aim to put your music, podcast, commercial, or audiobook On The Map. Plain and simple. What sets us aside from other labels is that we believe artists should be able to appreciate the value of their labor. Signing with us means you've joined a collective of artist and creators who believe in a better industry for the people. So lets put you On The Map.

The Studio

Our in-house production studio is designed and equipped to make that happen. While we focus primarily with vocals, we also offer instrumental tracking as well. We run sessions using AKG, Apple, Universal Audio, Avid, and Yamaha hardware with Native Instruments, IK Media, Celemony, Izotope, and Avid softwares. In the past we have worked on oral essays, studio albums, live shows, and music for film. We look forward to bringing your music or audio to life.

The Engineer

Eli, also known by his stagename: RYLTK (Pronounced: Real Talk, aka Realize Your Life Through Knowledge), has been making music for over 20 years, and producing audio for 10 years. As a graduate of SAE's audio technology program in CA, he moved to CT where he started On The Map Productions. When in the studio, he tracks, edits, mixes, and masters, also while being behind the mic delivering his word. You can find him on facebook, instagram, and twitter, SoundCloud, and YouTube.

What We Do

Plain and simple, our aim is to put you on the map. We also offer remote podcast hosting. We will record and edit your podcast from any corner of the world to our studio in Connecticut. Are you a Rapper with a beat and vocals to record, lets track it! We aim to make your music radio ready. While we focus mainly on vocals for music, commercials, podcasts, ADR, we also repair audio.
