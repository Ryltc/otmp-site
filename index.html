The Studio

On The Map Productions LLC, also knmown as OTMP, is based in the Mill Plain of Waterbury CT. Our vocal studio is designed to be a one stop shop. We track, edit, mix, and master any and all vocals. We run sessions using AKG, Apple, Universal Audio, Avid, and Yamaha hardware with Native Instruments, IK Media, Celemony, Izotope, and Avid softwares. In the past we have worked on oral essays, studio albums, live shows, and music for film. We look forward to bringing your music or audio to life, as we strive to put you On The Map.

The Engineer

Eli, also known by stagename: RYLTK (Pronounced: Real Talk, or Realize Your Life Through Knowledge), has been playing music for over 20 years, and producing audio for 10 years. As a graduate of SAE's audio technology program in CA, he moved to CT where he started On The Map Productions. When in the studio, he tracks, edits, mixes, and masters, also while being behind the mic delivering his word. You can find him on facebook, instagram, and twitter, SoundCloud, and YouTube.
